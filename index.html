About

Dan Seasonal Services is a local, student-run business providing reliable year-round outdoor property services. From snow removal in winter to lawn mowing and seasonal cleanups, every job is handled with care, consistency, and clear communication.

Our Story

Dan Seasonal Services started as a door to door snow shoveling activity for cold snowy days. I did this in my neighborhood for years before expanding my services and range. What started as something to burn time on days off of school became a still-growing project of learning to run and manage a business, while giving my amazing neighbors nothing but the best!
